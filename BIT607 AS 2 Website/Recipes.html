Rewena Bread

Ingredients:

Dried yest 1Tbsp, Sugar 2Tbsp, Warm Water 1cup, Potato mashed 1cup, Olive oil 2Tbsp, Flour 2cups, Salt 1tsp.

Method


  1. In a small bowl combine the yeast and sugar.
  2. Place the water into another bowl and sprinkle over the yeast and sugar mix. Let it stand for 10 minutes until frothy.
  3. In another bowl, combine the mashed potato and olive oil.
  4. Into a large bowl place the flour and salt, and stir through the potato.
  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry, combining well. Tip onto a lightly floured bench, and knead for 5 minutes until well combined.
  6. Grease and line the base of a 22cm cake tin. Place the dough into the tin then into a warm place for 1 hour until it has doubled in size.
  7. Preheat an oven to 180°C (fan-forced 160°C).
  8. Bake the bread for 45 minutes or until it sounds hollow when tapped. Remove from the oven and move onto a wire rack to cool.
