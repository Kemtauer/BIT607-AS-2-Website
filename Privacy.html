THE HAUKAI RESTAURANT PRIVACY STATEMENT

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • name
  • contact information
  • location
  • computer or network
  • interactions with us

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • process online bookings
  • identify people who are dining at our restaurant

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter contact details , we'll be

unable to process online and phone bookings.

We keep your information safe by storing it on encrypted files and only senior staff can

access it .

We keep your information for two years at which point we securely destroy it by securely

erasing all digital files.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to

ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your

information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at thehaukaurestaurant@gmail.com,

or (09) 401 6254, or 55 Kerikeri Road, Kerekeri, 0230, New Zealand.
